Title: Factors for Project Success

Outcomes and value

- Business KPIs achieved, user adoption/retention, NPS/CSAT, revenue or cost savings.

Delivery and quality

- Predictable delivery (on-time, scope managed), low escaped defects, high test coverage.
- DORA metrics: deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, MTTR.

Reliability and security

- SLOs met (availability/latency), security posture (vulns closed, audits passed), compliance.

Team health and sustainability

- Clear ownership, low toil, good on-call experience, documentation, learning culture.

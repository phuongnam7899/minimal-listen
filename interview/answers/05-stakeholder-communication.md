Title: Keeping Stakeholders Informed

Cadence and artifacts

- Single roadmap with quarterly goals and monthly milestones.
- Biweekly demos; weekly status with risks, decisions, and upcoming changes.
- Release notes per deployment; public changelog for customer-facing changes.
- Dashboards: DORA metrics, uptime/SLOs, incident history, customer feedback.

Clarity and alignment

- RACI for decisions; ADRs for architecture; PRDs for features; acceptance criteria defined.
- Maintain risk register; explicit trade-offs and dependencies; change management log.

Tools

- Ticketing (Jira/Azure Boards), docs (Confluence/Notion), dashboards (Grafana/Datadog), Slack/Teams.
